siftr is a research platform for the cutting edge

what we're building

we think research deserves more than 140 characters.
we're building a platform to share research with the world.
no paywalls, no nonsense.

  • request reviews and feedback on your preliminary work
  • push beyond the PDF to experiment with interactive publishing formats
  • discuss new research directions with soon-to-be collaborators
  • track versions of your work as your research evolves
  • share your work with the world

how to contribute

sign up to gain access to the alpha version of our platform.
from there you can offer feedback and request new features.
